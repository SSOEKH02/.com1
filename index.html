About Sanjay Soekhoe

Sanjay Soekhoe is a professional strength and conditioning coach and consultant for athletes. Besides being the founder of boxing school IboSan, he also has a vast experience in many sports.

Being a big advocate of gaining knowledge, Sanjay is an avid reader to gain the know-how of training. He will always be active in the world of sports, as it is his biggest passion in life. The ever continuing pursuit of the perfect performance.

Sanjay has been involved in the strength and conditioning industry for years now and is one of only a handful of Westside Barbell Certified coaches in the world.
